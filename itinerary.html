Exploring Pura and Sacred Sites

Tirta Empul Temple

Spiritual Journey

Visit Pura Tirta Empul

Embark on your spiritual journey with a visit to Pura Tirta Empul, a sacred water temple located in Tampaksiring. As you step into the temple complex, you'll be greeted by the soothing sounds of gushing springs and the scent of fragrant incense. Witness devotees partaking in the purification rituals at the holy springs, believed to have healing properties.

Pura Jati Segara

Continue your spiritual journey with a visit to Pura Jati Segara, a serene coastal temple located in the village of Beraban, Tabanan. Nestled beside the shimmering shores of Bali's southwest coast, this picturesque temple is dedicated to Dewa Baruna, the Balinese god of the sea.

Mother Temple of Besakih

Explore the grandeur of the Mother Temple of Besakih, Bali's largest and most significant Hindu temple complex. Nestled on the slopes of Mount Agung, Bali's highest and holiest mountain, this awe-inspiring temple is a sacred pilgrimage site for Balinese Hindus.

Pura Kehen

Complete your spiritual journey with a visit to Pura Kehen, a sacred temple nestled in the lush foothills of Bangli, Bali. Known as the "Jewel of Bangli," this ancient temple is renowned for its exquisite architecture, rich history, and profound spiritual significance.

Note:

We would recommend you to wear a sarong and sash when visiting the temple. Also remember to dress modestly and behave respectfully during your visit.
